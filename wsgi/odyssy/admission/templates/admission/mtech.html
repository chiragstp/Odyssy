M.Tech. Admissions



Admissions to the M.Tech. (CSE) Program is made through the Centralized Counselling for M.Tech./M.Arch./M.Plan./M.Des. admissions (CCMT). Official notification of the admissions is given wide publicity through national dailies during March-April every year. CCMT is on-line admission process for NITs and many IIITs including other national level institutions. It is a merit based admission considering valid GATE score and qualifying degree norms as declared by the CCMT. A candidate may participate in the CCMT admissions process through the official admission portal of the CCMT

Details about cutoffs, participating institutions, seat matrix, and more are available at CCMT 2018.
For any queries or details related to MTech admissions, e-mail us at pgadmission@iiitvadodara.ac.in.
